4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data with third-party service providers (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure; use at your own risk.

6. Your Choices

You can disable analytics sharing in the App Settings. To delete your usage data, contact us at hello@w8log.com.
